Hi there! I am Dayu Xia (夏大雨), a senior student majoring in Mechanical Engineering @ [ZJUI, Zhejiang University](https://zjui.intl.zju.edu.cn/en) and [Dept. of MechSE, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://mechse.illinois.edu/) through their joint degree program. I have the honor to be advised by Prof. Liangjing Yang, Prof. Hua Chen from ZJU, and [Prof. Dušan M. Stipanović](https://ise.illinois.edu/directory/profile/dusan) from UIUC.

In Fall 2026, I will join [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/) for my M.Eng. degree in Big Data Technology and Engineering. Previously, I interned at [IM Motors](https://www.immotors.com/global/en/), working on VLMs in autonomous driving.

From June 2024 to April 2025, I worked with [Prof. Dušan M. Stipanović](https://ise.illinois.edu/directory/profile/dusan), [Dr. Srikar Annamraju](https://iiitdm.ac.in/people/faculty/srikar@iiitdm.ac.in), and [Mr. Harris Nisar](https://harrisnisar.github.io/) from [CSL, UIUC](https://csl.illinois.edu/) on a robotic trail maker system facilitating upper-limb rehabilitation. In April 2025, we wrapped up our work: [Robotic Trail Maker Platform for Rehabilitation in Neurological Conditions: Clinical Use Cases](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2504.19230).

## About me

My research interests include VLAs and RL-based control in robotic learning. I am especially interested in building efficient and safe end-to-end systems for robots and autonomous vehicles.

I was born and raised in Shanghai, China. I enjoy swimming, music, photography, and traveling.
